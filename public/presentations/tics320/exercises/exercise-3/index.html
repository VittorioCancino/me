Each appointment connects one client with one staff member and records when the service takes place.

Important Notice

Natural Join Is Not Always the Right Move

  • In this schema, some attributes intentionally share names across relations because we want them to match, but other same-named attributes should not be matched.
  • In those cases, a natural join can remove tuples that we still need. When that happens, use Cartesian product and then enforce only the matches that actually belong in the query.
